Privacy Policy — Viral Engine

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Viral Engine collects and uses information in connection with our automated content publishing application. Viral Engine helps creators publish short-form videos to social media platforms including TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

1. Information We Collect

Viral Engine collects only the information necessary to operate the Service, including:

  • OAuth access tokens for connected social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram)
  • Video content metadata (titles, descriptions, upload timestamps, hashtags)
  • Basic account identifiers (channel IDs, usernames, display names)
  • Usage data such as upload history and performance metrics

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, financial data, or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by Viral Engine is used solely to:

  • Authenticate and publish content to your connected social media platforms on your behalf
  • Track upload history and monitor content performance
  • Maintain and improve Service functionality
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Viral Engine.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. OAuth tokens and account data are stored securely and used exclusively for publishing content on your behalf. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Storage & Security

All credentials and tokens are stored on secured servers with industry-standard security practices. We do not store your social media passwords. Access tokens are encrypted at rest and in transit. You may revoke access at any time through your social media account settings.

5. Third-Party Platforms

Viral Engine interacts with TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram APIs. Your use of these platforms is also subject to their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you. You may revoke Viral Engine's access at any time by disconnecting the app from your social media account settings. To request data deletion or for any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of Viral Engine after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, please contact us via GitHub.
